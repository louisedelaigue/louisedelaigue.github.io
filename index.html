about

I am currently involved in a joint PhD programme with the NWO-NIOZ Royal Netherlands Institute for Sea Research and Utrecht University, supervised by Dr. Matthew Humphreys.

My research investigates ocean-going, observational carbonate chemistry with a focus on how the ocean responds to increasing anthropogenic CO2 emissions, especially in under-sampled regions such as the southern Indian Ocean and Pacific Ocean.
